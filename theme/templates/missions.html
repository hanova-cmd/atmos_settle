Modern Island

Designing sustainable, self-sufficient colony for humanity's future beyond Earth

Project Vision

To create a comprehensive design for a sustainable space settlement that addresses the challenges of long-term human habitation beyond Earth, serving as a blueprint for future interplanetary civilization.

Technical Objectives

  • Design Establish a Permanent, Self-Sustaining Habitat
  • Achieve Full Resource Closure
  • Support Independent Population Growth:
  • Leverage Deep-Space ISRU

Human Factors

  • Ensure population capacity is 200 000
  • Make Operational Autonomy > 100 years
  • Create 100% Food and Water Self-Sufficiency
  • Have On-site Power Source

Technical Approach

Energy

Energy source at 30-50 AU capable of providing continuous multi-megawatt baseload power

Gravity

Via constructing a stable, low-eccentricity (near-circular) orbit around the KBO

Protection

Protects population from Galactic Cosmic Rays via passive mass shielding

Agriculture

Building the 12-Species Core Plant Suite
