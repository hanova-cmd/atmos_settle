Our Mission

Designing sustainable, self-sufficient habitats for humanity's future beyond Earth

Project Vision

To create a comprehensive design for a sustainable space settlement that addresses the challenges of long-term human habitation beyond Earth, serving as a blueprint for future interplanetary civilization.

Technical Objectives

  • Design closed-loop life support systems
  • Develop sustainable energy solutions
  • Create radiation protection systems
  • Design efficient space agriculture

Human Factors

  • Ensure psychological well-being
  • Design comfortable living spaces
  • Create community structures
  • Plan for cultural sustainability

Technical Approach

Energy

Solar & nuclear power systems

Recycling

Closed-loop resource management

Protection

Radiation & micro-meteoroid shielding

Agriculture

Hydroponic & aeroponic farming
