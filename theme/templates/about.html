About Team Atmos

Meet the passionate high school students behind Team Atmos - young innovators shaping the future of space settlement.

Our Story

Team Atmos was formed by a group of ambitious high school students who share a common vision: to make humanity a multi-planetary species. What started as a classroom discussion about space exploration evolved into a dedicated team competing in the prestigious AAERO Space Settlement Competition.

We believe that our generation has the responsibility and creativity to solve the challenges of extraterrestrial living. Through innovation, collaboration, and hard work, we're turning science fiction into science reality.
