AAERO Competition

The premier international competition for designing humanity's future in space

About AAERO

The AAERO Space Settlement Competition challenges students worldwide to design comprehensive plans for self-sustaining human habitats in space. This prestigious competition brings together the brightest young minds to solve the most pressing challenges of extraterrestrial living.

Teams must address everything from life support and radiation protection to psychological well-being and economic sustainability in their designs.

International Reach

Teams from over 50 countries

Judging Criteria

Technical Feasibility 25%
Innovation & Creativity 20%
Sustainability 20%
Human Factors 15%
Presentation 10%

Key Requirements

  • Minimum 50-year habitat viability
  • Support for 100+ residents
  • Closed-loop life support systems
  • Radiation protection design
  • Economic sustainability plan
